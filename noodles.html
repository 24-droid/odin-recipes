Noodles Recipe:

Description:

Vegetable noodles is a healthy Chinese inspired dish where cooked noodles are stir fried with lots of vegetables. Further the dish is seasoned with a splash of sauces, vinegar and black pepper to up the taste and flavor.

    Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Sauces
  • Vegetables

    Steps:

  1. Boil the noodles in atleast 5 cups of water
  2. While the noodles cook rinse the vegetables quickly in the running water
  3. When the noodles get cooked drain out the water,Rinse the cooked noodles with cold water
  4. Fry the vegetables,prepare the sauces by adding 1 tablespoon soya souce,1 tablespoon vinegar, 1 tablespoon hot sauce in a bowl.
  5. Heat a pan add 1 garlic and 2 chilli and heat it on the highest flame
  6. Fry the garlic and chilli untill it smells good.
  7. Next add all the veggies
  8. Fry stirring often for 2-3 minutes.
  9. Add the cooked noodles.Add salt with caution since the sauces has salt present in it.
  10. Toss and mix the noodles for 2 mins.Turn of the flame
