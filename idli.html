Idli Recipe:

Description:

Idli or idly is a type of savoury rice cake, originating from South India, popular as a breakfast food in Southern India and in Sri Lanka. The cakes are made by steaming a batter consisting of fermented black lentils (de-husked) and rice.

    Ingredients:

  • Urad Dal
  • Chana Dal
  • Meethi Seeds
  • Rice
  • Poha
  • Cold Water

    Steps:

    To make Idli Batter:

  1. Add 0.5 cup Urad dal,2 tablespoon Chana Dal,0.5 tablesppon methi seeds to a large bowl. Add 0.5 cup raw rice to another bowl.
  2. Drain all the water and add the soaked ingredients to a mixer grinder or blender.
  3. Grind it into a smooth batter by adding enough batter.
  4. Combine the two in a large bowl,keep it in a warm place for 5-6 hours.Hence your batter is ready.

    To make Idli:

  1. Greash or brush the idli mould with oil
  2. Gently and lightly add the batter
  3. Add 2-2.5 cups water and heat the water in the pressure cooker.add the idli mould to the cooker.
  4. Steam the idlis for 12-15 minutes,Hence after doing thisyour idlis is ready.
