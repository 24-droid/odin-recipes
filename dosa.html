Dosa Recipe:

Description:

A dosa is a thin, savoury crepe in South Indian cuisine made from a fermented batter of ground white gram and rice. Dosas are served hot, often with chutney and sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew). Dosas are popular in South Asia as well as around the world.

    Ingredients:

  • Urad Dal
  • Chana Dal
  • Meethi Seeds
  • Rice
  • Poha
  • Cold Water

    Steps:

    To make Dosa Batter:

  1. Add 0.5 cup Urad dal,2 tablespoon Chana Dal,0.5 tablesppon methi seeds to a large bowl. Add 0.5 cup raw rice to another bowl.
  2. Drain all the water and add the soaked ingredients to a mixer grinder or blender.
  3. Grind it into a smooth batter by adding enough batter.
  4. Combine the two in a large bowl,keep it in a warm place for 5-6 hours.Hence your batter is ready.

    To make Dosa:

  1. Heat a tawa,Pour about 0.5 cup batter to the center of the tawa.
  2. Gently Spread the batter from the center to the sides of the tawa
  3. Drizzle about 1 tablespoon oil on the edges of the dosa and also at the top of it.
  4. Use a spatulla to gently lift the dosa.And flip it over.Cook the other side for 40 seconds. Hence your Dosa is Ready.
